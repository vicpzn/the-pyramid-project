    HOW TO PLAY

    The Pyramid Project is a game based on randomization and is played in 4 rounds. Each round, you will draw a random card and have some guesses to make:

    1. Guess the color of the card. +10 points for a correct guess.
    2. Guess if the value of the card is higher of inferior than the value of your first card. +20 points for a correct guess. +10 points if the card is equal to the first one.
    3. Guess if the value of the card is inside or outside the interval of your two first cards. +30 points for a correct guess. +15 points if the drawn card is equal to one of the two first cards.
    4. Guess the symbol of the card. +40 points for a correct guess.

    The objective is to reach 100 points!
