INSTRUCTIONS

The Pyramid Project is a game based on randomization and is divided in 4 rounds. Each round, you will draw a random card and have some guesses to make:

  1. Guess the color of the card.
  2. Guess if the value of the card is higher of inferior compared to your first card.
  3. Guess if the value of the card is in or out the interval of your two current cards.
  4. Guess the family of the card.
