    INSTRUCTIONS

    The Pyramid Project is a game based on randomization and is divided in 4 rounds. Each round, you will draw a random card and have some guesses to make:

    1. Guess the color of the card. +10 points for a correct guess.
    2. Guess if the value of the card is higher of inferior compared to your first card. +20 points for a correct guess.
    3. Guess if the value of the card is in or out the interval of your two current cards. +30 points for a correct guess.
    4. Guess the family of the card. +40 points for a correct guess.

    The objective is to reach 100 points!
